MARC21 Utilities

  1. MARC21 Validation XSL stylesheets to validate MARC21 bibliographic and authority records. The stylesheets are generated automatically as a result of parsing Library of Congress MARC Format pages (http://www.loc.gov/marc)

  2. Mixed Greek Latin mixedgrlat.php checks for words with mixed greek latin characters, produces areport and suggests corrections.

  3. UNIMARC to MARC21 Usemarcon UNIMARC 2 MARC21 conversion
